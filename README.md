# 🏘️ Community Hero
### AI-Powered Civic Intelligence & Community Issue Resolution Platform

> Transforming community issue reporting from reactive complaints into intelligent, collaborative, and knowledge-driven civic management using **Google AI**.

---

# 🚀 Overview

Community Hero is an AI-powered platform that enables residents of gated communities, residential societies, campuses, and smart communities to collaboratively report, validate, resolve, and learn from civic issues.

Instead of simply storing complaints, Community Hero builds a continuously growing **Community Knowledge Base**, preventing duplicate reports, recommending historical solutions, and rewarding responsible citizens.

The platform combines **Google Gemini Vision**, **Google Embeddings**, geospatial intelligence, community verification, and gamification into one unified ecosystem.

---

Modern communities suffer from:

- Duplicate issue reports

Community Hero **understands, verifies, learns, and improves** with every issue.

# Impact

Community Hero transforms civic issue management from a complaint system into an **AI-powered collaborative intelligence platform**.

By combining **Google Gemini Vision**, **Google Embeddings**, geospatial intelligence, and community participation, it reduces duplicate reports, accelerates issue resolution, preserves institutional knowledge, and empowers residents to actively improve their communities.

Rather than simply resolving today's problems, Community Hero continuously learns from every interaction—making each community progressively smarter, more efficient, and more resilient.
